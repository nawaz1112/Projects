Web Conference Licenses

Licenses for Individual Conferences
Currently, EasyChair supports four different kinds of licenses for individual conferences (free, professional, executive and group license). Their features, comparison and prices are shown in the table below.


When you initialize a new conference, it will be automatically given the free license. To apply for a non-free license, chairs should follow menu items (Conference acronym)->License Management.
When your license application has been approved, we will issue you an invoice. You can pay your license fee online by a credit card or by wire transfer. The invoice will contain payment details, including the link to the online payment We page, our bank details and (when applicable) our and your VAT numbers.
Group License for a Conference If your conference requires separate installations for parts of this conference (such as tracks, special sessions or doctoral consortium), the only license you can use is a group license. Each of these parts will have access to all features of the executive license, but the prices is different (and are based on total number of submissions for the whole group). Note that the group license does not apply for a group of conferences, only to parts of the same conference.
We can make an exception for workshops. If you have an independent workshop run together with a conference, we can allow it to have a separate license. Note that it is entirely up to us to decide whether your workshop is independent or not.
Professional Conference Organizers and Institutions If you are a company or an institution where conference organization or working with conferences is part of your activities, our individual or group licenses do not apply to your conferences.
We have special professional conference organizer and institution licenses. The prices are individual and are based on a per-conference fee and a per-submission fee. You should contact us to arrange such a license.
Please note that we do not allow to resell our services or consult on how to use EasyChair without our express permission to any organizations or individual whose professional duties include conference organisation or who are paid for doing so.
